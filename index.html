F.A.Q.

CRM (Customer Relationship Management) in commercial real estate businesses is a tool that organizes and manages client interactions. It assists in contact management, lead management & tracking, pipeline management, task automation, deal tracking, back office management, and improving customer service for real estate professionals.

Composer is a world-class Commercial Real Estate CRM tool and a report generation tool that besides advanced functionality and flexible interface design fully integrates with AscendixRE CRM and has a reasonable price. Composer is included in the $99/user/mo AscendixRE Enterprise plan, which gives you access to both advanced CRM functionality and a report generation tool. No extra fees.
